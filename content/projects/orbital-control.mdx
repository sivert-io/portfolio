We partnered with a space logistics startup to design the command surface for their orbital deployment platform. The brief called for a visual language that felt like a cinematic sci-fi UI but remained production-ready and keyboard accessible.

### Highlights

1. **WebGL trajectory map** with shader-driven trails that respond to live telemetry.
2. **Handoff-ready design system** with tokenized motion specs for engineers.
3. **Automated stress testing** harness that simulated solar flare events and comms dropouts.

![Mission timeline interface showing anomaly alerts](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1451187580459-43490279c0fa?auto=format&fit=crop&w=1400&q=80)

We shipped the MVP in under eight weeks and trained the operations crew with interactive walkthroughs built directly in the app. Latency decreased by 34% thanks to the progressive data loading patterns we introduced.
