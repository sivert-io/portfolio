## Hey, I'm Sivert 👋

I’m a developer and designer from Norway who loves turning ideas into real, working things. I’ve spent the past 11+ years building everything from automation tools and web apps to open-source platforms and indie games. My work often lives where **code, creativity, and sustainability** meet.

I currently work as a **Front-end Developer at Norsk Tipping**, focusing on accessible, high-quality user experiences for millions of players. Alongside work, I’m pursuing a **Master’s in Interaction Design at NTNU**, exploring how digital design can be both _beautiful and sustainable_.

![Sketching interface flows on a light table](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1545239351-1141bd82e8a6?auto=format&fit=crop&w=1400&q=80)

### What drives me

- **Open source as community.** I love building tools that people can use, learn from, and improve — like [**Gryt**](https://gryt.chat), an open-source voice chat platform.
- **Sustainability as performance.** Great design shouldn’t come at the planet’s expense. My research and [Eurekaprize-winning](https://www.universitetsavisa.no/eurekaprisen-gjovik-ntnu/gjovik-disse-vant-arets-eurekapris/387933) bachelor thesis explored practical ways to make the web greener.
- **Learning by doing.** Whether it’s training an AI model to catch shiny Pokémon or automating CS LAN tournaments with [**MatchZy Auto Tournament**](https://github.com/sivert-io/matchzy-auto-tournament), I’m happiest when experimenting.

### How I work

I design and prototype quickly — validating ideas early, testing with users, and refining with real data. Once the vision feels right, I bring it to life with React, TypeScript, and a focus on sustainable performance and accessible motion.

I like to think of code as a design material: something expressive, tactile, and full of personality when used with care.

### Outside the editor

When I’m not coding, I’m usually making music, streaming games, or wrenching on my **Golf GTI Edition 35** (you might catch it on [edition35.com](https://edition35.com) — if my home server’s awake 😅).  
I also love photography, video editing, and the occasional late-night Godot jam session.
