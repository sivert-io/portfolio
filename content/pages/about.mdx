## Hey, I'm Sivert

I craft immersive interfaces and technical storytelling for forward-thinking teams. My sweet spot lives where product strategy, interaction design, and animation intersect. I obsess over the subtle details: easing curves that feel natural, typography that guides without shouting, and systems that scale gracefully without losing personality.

![Sketching interface flows on a light table](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1545239351-1141bd82e8a6?auto=format&fit=crop&w=1400&q=80)

### What drives me

- **Motion as language.** Thoughtful transitions help people build trust and intuition with a product.
- **Full-stack empathy.** I move comfortably between Figma, TypeScript, and shaders, translating constraints into opportunities.
- **Accessible polish.** I see no trade-off between sweeping visuals and inclusive experiences.

### How I work

I lead discovery, prototype rapidly, and validate ideas with real users whenever possible. Once we're confident in the direction, I help ship the production implementation—usually in React, powered by frameworks like Vite or Next.js, with design systems tailored for the brand.

### Outside the editor

When I'm not animating pixels, you can find me capturing analog photos of Scandinavian coastlines, tinkering with modular synth patches, or curating playlists for late-night creative jams.
